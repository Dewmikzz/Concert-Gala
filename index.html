About The Event

An Evening of Music & Hope

Concert Gala 2026 – Rhythm of Hopes is SDCKL's first-ever Concert Gala and Alumni Gathering, bringing together students, alumni, lecturers, parents, and the public.

Inspired by the elegance of a Met Gala, the event celebrates Tamil music, student talent, and community spirit while supporting charitable causes.
